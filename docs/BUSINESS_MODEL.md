# Soulo — Business Model

## 1. Pricing

| Tier | Price | Features | Value Prop |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | 7 entries (trial) | Try before you buy |
| **Monthly** | $9.99 | Unlimited entries, all insights | < 1 coffee/day |
| **Annual** | $79.99 | Everything + encrypted backup | 33% discount ($6.67/mo) |
| **Family** | $14.99/mo | 5 users + elderly cognitive monitoring | Protect aging parents |

## 2. Revenue Model

```
Y1 Target: 1,000 paid users @ $9.99/mo = $10,000/mo MRR
Y2 Target: 10,000 paid users @ $9.99/mo = $100,000/mo MRR
Y3 Target: 50,000 paid users @ $8.50 avg/mo = $425,000/mo MRR
```

### Assumptions
- Conversion rate (free → paid): 15% (industry avg for health apps: 12-18%)
- Monthly churn: 5% (wellness apps avg: 6-8%)
- Annual plan adoption: 30% (lowers effective churn)
- Family plan adoption: 10% of paid users

## 5. Virality Mechanics

### Built-in Sharing
- **Insight Cards**: Long-press any insight → shareable image card
  - "Soulo told me I'm avoiding this conversation. Maybe I should have it."
  - Branded with Soulo watermark → free installs
- **Year in Review**: Annual report (like Spotify Wrapped)
  - "You cried 12 times this year. You laughed 847 times. You mentioned quitting 43 times. You're still here."
  - Highly shareable, emotional, drives referrals
- **Referral Program**: 1 month free for friend who journals 7 days

### Network Effects
- **Family Plan**: "Your mom's voice patterns changed. Suggest she check in."
- **Therapist Sharing**: Opt-in share of patterns with therapist
  - "Your patient showed up to therapy. Here's what happened between sessions."
- **Cognitive Decline Monitoring**: Adult children subscribe for elderly parents

## 6. Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Weakness | Soulo Advantage |
|---|---|---|
| **Therapy ($150/session)** | Expensive, 1h/week, biased | $10/mo, unlimited, zero judgment |
| **Day One** | No AI, just storage | Pattern detection, biomarkers, insights |
| **ChatGPT** | No long-term memory, one-off | 365 days of continuous self-knowledge |
| **Moodpath / Daylio** | Manual mood tracking, superficial | Passive voice analysis, deep biomarkers |
| **BetterHelp** | $65/week, therapist matching | Cheaper, daily, remembers everything |
| **Nothing** (no competitor does this) | — | Zero config voice analysis + pattern engine |

## 8. Funding Strategy

This is designed as a **bootstrappable solo founder project**.

| Phase | Cost | Funding Source |
|---|---|---|
| MVP (3 months) | $0 (your time) | Self |
| Launch | $0 (organic) | Self |
| First 100 users | $0-500/mo | Revenue |
| First 1,000 users | $1,000-3,000/mo | Revenue |
| Scale (10K+ users) | $5,000-10,000/mo | Revenue or Angel |

**No VC needed.** At 1,000 paid users ($10K MRR), you're profitable. At 10K ($100K MRR), you're a lifestyle business. At 50K+, you have options.
