Title: Soulo: AI Voice Journal
Short Description: Private AI voice journal with emotional insights
Full Description:
Soulo is a private, on-device AI voice journal that analyzes your voice patterns to help you understand your emotional well-being.

HOW IT WORKS
Just speak naturally for 3 minutes a day. Soulo transcribes your voice locally using Whisper, analyzes biomarkers like pitch, energy, and speech rate, and surfaces patterns in your emotional state over time.

ALL ON-DEVICE PRIVACY
Your voice data never leaves your phone. No account required, no cloud uploads, no analytics SDKs. Everything is encrypted and stored locally.

KEY FEATURES
• Voice journaling with automatic transcription
• Voice biomarker analysis (pitch, energy, speech rate, jitter, shimmer)
• Emotion detection from voice patterns
• Cognitive drift tracking with Cohen's d effect size
• Behavior prediction and early warning signals
• Therapeutic summary generation for sharing with your provider
• Daily insights and personalized recommendations
• Decision tracking and outcome analysis
• End-to-end encryption (AES-GCM per entry)
• Biometric lock (Face ID / fingerprint)
• 7-day free trial, then $9.99/month or $79.99/year
